## 共用 STYLE BASELINE

> Cute voxel-art icon in the style of friendly children's sandbox-block game. Single subject centered on a clean transparent or soft pastel background. Bright cheerful palette (grass green, sky blue, sunshine yellow, warm orange, dirt brown). Soft cel-shading, kid-friendly, optimistic mood. Designed for a small icon display (~80-120px on web).

## 共用 NEGATIVE PROMPT

> Avoid: any text, letters, numbers, words, logos, brand names, Minecraft trademarks, scary themes, dark colors, photorealistic, blurry, low quality, 3D photorealism, complex backgrounds.
